🌱 About Moringa

What is Moringa? — முருங்கை என்றால் என்ன?

Everything you need to know about Moringa oleifera — the miracle drumstick tree

Moringa (moringa oleifera) is a fast-growing tree native to South Asia, popularly known as the drumstick tree or murungai keerai (முருங்கைக் கீரை) in Tamil Nadu. Every part of the moringa tree — its moringa leaves, moringa seeds, moringa flower, moringa fruit and moringa oil — has documented nutritional and medicinal value.


Moringa scientific name: Moringa oleifera. It belongs to the Moringaceae family and is often called the "miracle tree" or "tree of life." In Hindi it is called sahjan or drumstick, and moringa meaning in Hindi refers to its long pod-like fruit resembling a drumstick.


Moringa Powder Benefits

  • Rich source of moringa protein — contains all 9 essential amino acids
  • High in Vitamin A, Vitamin C, calcium and iron — moringa leaves benefits surpass many superfoods
  • Supports moringa for weight loss by boosting metabolism and reducing cravings
  • Moringa benefits for men — improves energy, stamina and hormonal health
  • Moringa tea benefits — calming antioxidant drink for daily wellness
  • Moringa oil benefits — excellent for skin, hair and joint health
  • Helps manage blood sugar — often used as moringa medicine alongside a healthy diet

Moringa Side Effects

Moringa is generally safe when taken in recommended doses (1–2 tsp per day). Moringa side effects are rare but may include mild digestive discomfort if consumed in excess. Pregnant women should avoid moringa root or bark — consult your doctor before use.
